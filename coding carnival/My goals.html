My Goals for 2022




For a long time, I wanted to change up the way I am seen, and improve overall.
Here are my top 5 goals I am planning to achieve by the end of the year:



  1. Get rid of social anxiety


    2. This is my No.1 goal for a good reason. I have been afraid to,
    participate in any social event for as long as I was in school. Instead
    of staying backstage in a school of many opportunities, I want to step out
    of my safe zone and be seen as a brave student, not a shy one. I want to
    finally start joining in clubs and extracurricular activities once and for all.


  2. Get better at creativity, and get recognised


    3. Perhaps many do not know this, but I am quite fond of dancing, singing,
    anime drawing, and anything that allows me to express creativity. My goal is
    to get regonised by my skills, and not get scared to do so. I would like to
    start animating while doing digital art as well.


  3. Have more self-respect and feel "beautiful"


    4. After I started getting puberty, I started to feel imperior towards other
    students due to my acne, extremely messy hair, and lack of self-respect. From 2022,
    I really want to aim changing how I feel like right now, and look after myself
    more often. I also plan on doing more workouts to get the body shape I want. Maybe
    then, I would stop feeling somewhat 'miserable' with the way I look.


  4. Find out my true personality


    5. When I started making many friends in year 5, I realised that I acted differetly
    towards all of them, and that I really didn't have anyone who linked with the way I
    act. This year, I want to find out what my personality is really like, and make friends
    with that personality accordingly. I do not want to feel confused and misguided by others
    anymore.


  5. Socialise more with my family


    6. In my years as a year 5-6 person, I barely communicated with my mum, dad, grandma and
    grandpa. Whenever i'm at home, I spend 85% of my time facing the screen. I really want to
    fix the way I act towards my family, because it really does affect the way I feel about
    myself.

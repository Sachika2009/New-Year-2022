What I am excited for in 2022




Listed down here are some things I am pretty excited about in 2022:



  1. New Friends


    2. Ever since grade 4 started for me, I realised that most of my
    friends have somewhat changed. A new school is like a new environment,
    and i am so excited to finally start fresh and pop my bubbled-up personality.


  2. New Environment


    3. In my public school, I was seen as the "shy" kid who didn't really
    participate in anything. Now, with a new environment, I can change up
    how I am seen as, and improve as a person altogether.


  3. More Opportunities


    4. I had a passion of doing visual arts and anything creative in general
    for a long amount of time. tennis was also in my 'interest' category. With
    more opportunities, I can really develop and improve what I love to do, and
    try out many other passions i've had for a long time!


  4. My 13th Birthday


    5. In my years of being an 9-12 year old, I always wondered how it is like
    being a teenager. I am really excited about this birthday in particular
    'cuz I will be hosting it at a massive resteraunt! My best friends will finally
    be invited, along with the regularly invited guests.


  5. Watching my little brother grow


    6. This year, my brother, Sheehan, will be attending Kindergarten! Just like
    me, he will be put into a completly new place, and will have a completly different
    routine. I have a lot of hope for him to make new friends, do great in studies, and
    finally experience life with a thousand kids, like me!
